# Direction 2: Language Models and LLM Apps

## Good fit for students who like

- text
- chat interfaces
- assistants
- search
- automation

## Typical project ideas

- chatbot for a narrow domain
- summarizer
- classifier for messages or documents
- question-answering tool
- semantic search demo
- tool-calling assistant

## Strong starter tools

- Ollama
- Hugging Face Transformers
- Sentence Transformers
- Gradio
- FastAPI

## Good advice

- narrow the scope
- avoid trying to build a general chatbot for everything
- give the app one clear purpose
